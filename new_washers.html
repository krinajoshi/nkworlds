Plain Washers

Quick Overview

Plain washers are used to distribute the load of a threaded fastener. They can also be used as a spacer, spring (wave washer), wear pad, preload indicating device, locking device, and to reduce vibration (rubber washer). Similar to Flat Washers, with a general purpose.
