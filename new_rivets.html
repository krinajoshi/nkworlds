Inquire About Rivets

Blind Rivets (Pop Rivets)

Blind rivets are tubular fasteners with a mandrel through the center. They are used when access to the backside of the workpiece is limited. We offer various types including standard open-end, closed-end, multi-grip, and more.

Key Features:

  • Ideal for applications with no access to the rear side.
  • Available in various materials, head styles, and diameters.
  • Strong and reliable fastening.

Solid Rivets (e.g., Snap/Round/Flat Head)

Solid rivets are one of the oldest and most reliable types of fasteners, consisting of a solid shaft with a head on one end. They are installed by deforming the tail end with a hammer or rivet gun.

Key Features:

  • High strength and durability.
  • Permanent fastening solution.
  • Available in various head styles like Snap (Round), Countersunk (CSK), Flat.
