Elevator Bolts

Quick Overview

An Elevator Bolt has a flat, Plain or Counter sunk Head and Square neck that holds the bolts in place while the nut is tightened. Elevator bolts are mainly used with conveyer system. These bolts are mainly inserted in to hole that have already been drilled. In any elevator bolts It is very important to check out the bolt bonding and threading of the bolts. We are manufacturer of elevator bolts with the specialization in the same. Our production team will take care of the bonding and threading especially when they are being produced.
